Jollof Recipe

A Nigerian Delicacy, enjoyed all across the globe due to it's spicy and exploding flavors

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive Oil
  • Curry
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Perboil Your Rice
  2. Add oil to a hot pan
  3. Fry Onions
  4. Add tomato paste
  5. Add Curry and Thyme
  6. Add Pepper and fry till Tomato paste loses it's bitter taste
  7. Add Rice and water and Salt
  8. Cook till Rice is well done
