Egusi Recipe

A Nigerian Delicacy, most popular in the Southeastern region of the country, enjoyed all across the globe due to it's spicy and exploding flavors

  • Blended Melon seeds
  • Palm oil
  • Beef stock
  • Any desired protein
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Crafish
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Add oil to a hot pan
  2. Fry Onions
  3. Add blended Melon seeds with Crayfish
  4. Add seasoning
  5. Add Pepper and fry till Melon seeds loses it's bitter taste
  6. Add Rice beef stock and desired protein
  7. Add Salt and cook till desired taste
